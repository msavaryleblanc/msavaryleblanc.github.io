Maxime Savary-Leblanc, Ph.D.

Software Assistants UX researcher

University of Lille

Research center in Computer Science, Signal, and Automatic Control of Lille

I'm currently working with the CARBON team of the CRIStAL laboratory at the University of Lille. My research activities focus on model-driven software engineering assisted by artificial intelligence with an emphasis on related human factors. I defended my Ph.D. thesis on the 15th of December 2021 at the University of Lille. I obtained a Master of Science in Digital System Design and Engineering from the University of Lille in July 2018 after spending two years working as a mobile application software engineer in an IT major company.
